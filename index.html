How to Use This Trainer Effectively

This trainer is designed for verbatim recall. To extract maximum value from the current feature set, use the following operating procedure:

1. Treat Each Card as a Full Paragraph Unit

The card title is your cue. Before revealing anything, attempt a full cold recitation with all text hidden. Verbatim accuracy—not recognition—is the target metric.

2. Use Word-by-Word Reveal as an Audit Layer

Click or press space to reveal the text one word at a time. This surface is not for learning; it is for error detection. Identify where you substituted, reordered, or omitted words. These failures indicate weak segments that require targeted rehearsal.

3. Use Chunk Mode as a Mid-Level Support

The 7-word chunking tool provides a light scaffold. Use it only if you cannot maintain flow during a cold attempt. Forcing dependence on it reduces long-term retention.

4. Track Internal Progress, Not the Progress Bar

The progress bar reflects coverage, not mastery. The real milestone is: 100% correct recitation while the card is at 0% revealed.

5. Run Cards Forward in One Pass

Move sequentially through cards, overlapping cognitive load across paragraphs. This approximates a forward-run methodology.

6. Implement Reverse Runs Manually

Once forward recall is stable, manually run cards in reverse order (N → 1). This builds structural recall and eliminates flow-dependence.

7. Space Sessions According to Retention Best Practices

  • Initial session → Day 1 reactivation
  • Day 3–4 stability check
  • Day 7–10 consolidation
  • Day 30 retention audit

Spacing impacts consolidation more than session length.

8. For Stress-Proofing, Add Mild Load Externally

Because the trainer does not yet simulate stress, you must impose it yourself:

  • Stand or shift posture
  • Apply time pressure
  • Use partial cues (title only)
  • Attempt recitation immediately after a brief distraction

Avoid artificial irritation or sensory punishment—they degrade recall.

Upcoming Improvements & Planned Enhancements

This trainer will evolve from a recall surface into a full-stack memorization workflow. Planned enhancements follow the method we established:

1. Meaning-Unit Segmentation (Semantic Chunking)

Automatic division of each paragraph into 3–7 meaning-units, each representing a complete idea. Enables precision recall rather than word-only flow.

2. Cue Generation (Per-Unit Triggers)

3–5 word cues generated automatically for each meaning-unit to optimize retrieval under load.

3. Guided Progressive Rebuild

Structured 1→2→3→N recall passes with forced recall-first behavior, eliminating dependency on continual reveal.

4. Reverse-Run Mode

Built-in N→1 mode to stabilize order integrity and prevent dependence on forward rhythm.

5. Dropout Drills

Automated surfacing of weak segments based on your failure points. Focuses practice where cognitive payoff is highest.

6. Stress-Training Mode

Mild, controlled interference conditions (partial cues, time-limited prompts, simple dual-task demands). No flashing, no loud audio, no irritation.

7. Session Tracking & Spaced Scheduling

Local storage of performance, prompting reactivation at 24 hours, 72 hours, and long-term intervals.

8. Cold-Run Scoring

Formalized scoring for zero-visibility recitations, allowing objective measurement of mastery across sessions.
